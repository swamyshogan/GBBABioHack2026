A 7-year-old patient presents with developmental delays, muscle weakness, and cardiac abnormalities. Clinical exome sequencing was performed, and you've been given the BAM file. Find the causative variant and build a case for your diagnosis.

The Scenario

You have:

  • Aligned sequencing reads (BAM file) for the patient
  • Family history: Parents unaffected (suggesting recessive inheritance or de novo)
  • Phenotypes: developmental delay, hypertrophic cardiomyopathy, hypotonia
  • Previous genetic tests: Normal karyotype, negative for common muscular dystrophies

Your goal: Identify the most likely pathogenic variant and provide clinical evidence.
